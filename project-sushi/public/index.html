About Chef Geronimo

With more than 20 years of professional experience, Sushi Chef Geronimo brings a refined blend of tradition, artistry, and innovation to every dining experience.

Specializing in weddings, private events, and luxury catering, he has built a reputation for delivering elevated sushi that is as visually stunning as it is flavorful.

Chef Geronimo's approach combines time-honored Japanese techniques with modern creative flair, resulting in signature dishes that captivate guests and enhance any event.

From intimate omakase experiences to high-volume celebrations, Chef Geronimo is committed to creating memorable dining experiences with precision, hospitality, and craftsmanship.
